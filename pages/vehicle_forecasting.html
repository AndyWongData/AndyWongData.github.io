Used Vehicle Retail Price Forecasting

Between June 2023 and January 2024, I had the privilege of working with as a Data Science Intern with vAuto, a software company that provides inventory management and pricing solutions for automotive dealerships (ProfitTime GPS). A major component of ProfitTime GPS are the competitive pricing recommendations for dealerships. Dealerships catalog their vehicles into the software listing vehicle details (i.e. brand, condition, trim), and the software provides a pricing recommendation based on inputs.

Recommendations were often followed save for when dealers were given a recommendation lower than what they expected. This was a major pain point, and a priority to address. My internship project was centered on solving this problem. To help dealers feel more confidence in the ProfitTime GPS recommendations, I created a machine learning model to predict retail prices of used vehicles (15 - 60 days out).
